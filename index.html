About Me

CARPE DIEM!

Hello to ALL visitors! My name is Amir. I am currently learning coding to puruse a career in software development. I have absolutely NO EXPERIENCE in coding. Well, I'm lying, I took 2 very basic 8 week courses at Ivy Tech in Indiana. However, the vast majority of my working career has been in something completely opposite(truck driving). I think today's weather here in Chicago can probably attest to why I am looking for a career change! lol. I do have a B.A. in political science and criminal justice. I am a huge question asker(if that is a logical term). I like to problem solve and often get frustrated when people don't like to answer my million and one question. I thought, it pursuit of answers, I could apply that skill here in programming. I enjoy computers and looking at pretty websites, but eventually I would like to know how to put those things together! By this pre-work, I see that I am going to be pulling my hair out already!! no worries...I am up for the challenge! I am working on Windows operating system. I have no experience with macs, but intend to eventually fill my way around that as well. Outside of work I enjoy horror films and cooking with my LOVE.
